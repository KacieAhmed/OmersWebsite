Who We Are

Creating spaces where community thrives

SALAAM Spaces is an established initiative offering programs focused on young adults aged 18–35 in the Greater Toronto Area. We provide a welcoming environment for community members to engage in meaningful activities, build skills, and foster strong connections.

Our inclusive space welcomes people of all backgrounds and involvement levels — united by a shared desire to grow, connect, and serve.

Our Mission

Creating a safe, inclusive, and supportive space that fosters meaningful engagement — where programs enhance skills, connect peers, and deepen our connection to Islamic values.
